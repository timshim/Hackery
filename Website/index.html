Hackery

A clean, fast Hacker News client for iPhone, iPad, and Apple Vision Pro.

Available on iOS, iPadOS, and visionOS

Stay in the Loop

Browse the latest Hacker News stories in a clean, card-based layout. See scores, comment counts, and authors instantly. Tap any story to read in Safari's reader mode.

Never Lose a Story

Swipe to save stories for later. Your bookmarks sync across all your devices via iCloud, so you never lose track of what you want to read.

Follow the Conversation

Follow conversations with nested, threaded comments. Links are tappable and underlined. Discussions load in pages so the app stays fast, even on long threads.

Easy on the Eyes

Easy on the eyes, day or night. Hackery follows your system appearance with a carefully crafted dark theme.

Dive Deeper

Dive deep into discussions with beautifully rendered comment threads. Bookmark stories right from the comments view and open articles with a tap.
